ℹ️ Help - What Can It Do?

📱 Basic Usage

1. Enter From and To numbers (10 digits).

2. Click Start Call to trigger immediately.

⏱️ Auto Mode

Set your desired interval and turn Auto ON. The app will automatically trigger calls at regular intervals with countdown and progress ring.

🚫 Blocked Numbers

Click Manage Blocked Numbers to add or remove numbers. Blocked numbers cannot be used for calls.

🔄 Number Format

Numbers are automatically normalized. You can enter with or without +880 or leading zero.

Firebase automatically saves your blocked numbers so they persist even after closing the app.
